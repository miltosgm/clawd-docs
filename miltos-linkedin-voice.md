## Do's and Don'ts

### DO:
- Start with a hook that stops the scroll
- Use short paragraphs with breathing room
- Include a clear takeaway or CTA
- Reference real data/numbers when possible
- Keep it educational but opinionated

### DON'T:
- Use excessive emojis or hashtags
- Write long walls of text
- Be vague or wishy-washy
- Sound salesy or pushy
- Use corporate jargon
