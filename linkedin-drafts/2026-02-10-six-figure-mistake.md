**The setup:**

We were targeting premium e-commerce brands. High AOV. Wealthy audience.

Strategy: Luxury positioning + scarcity messaging.

Budget: $100K over 90 days.

Expected ROI: 3:1 minimum.

Actual ROI: 0.8:1 (we lost money).

**Where I went wrong:**

Not one thing. Multiple compounding mistakes:

1. **Assumed our model applied to this vertical**
   - Our system worked for SaaS
   - Doesn't mean it works for e-commerce
   - Different buyer psychology entirely

2. **Didn't validate before scaling**
   - Should have tested with $5K first
   - Should have waited for proof
   - Instead: went straight to $100K

3. **Got attached to the strategy**
   - Data said stop in week 4
   - I kept going "just one more week"
   - Sunk cost fallacy is real

4. **Ignored my instinct**
   - Early signals were wrong
   - Low quality leads
   - But I rationalized it away

**What I tell founders now:**
