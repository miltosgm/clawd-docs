## What is AEO?

**Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)** is the practice of optimizing your brand and content to be recommended by AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google's AI Overviews.

25-30% of research queries now start in AI tools instead of Google. If you're not showing up there, you're invisible to a growing segment of your market.
