### 2. **Phone Follow-Up on Warm Leads**
Research shows phone calls are #1 for getting clients but everyone avoids them.

**The play:**
- Take your existing email list / past inquiries
- Call them (yes, actually call)
- Simple script: "Hey, I was going through my contacts and realized we never connected. What's keeping you up at night with [marketing/ads/growth]?"

**Why it works:** 99% of your competitors won't do this. It's uncomfortable = low competition.

---

### 3. **AEO as a New Service Line**
The AEO market is exploding. No one in Cyprus is doing this. You can own this positioning.

**Data:**
- Zero-click searches up 3% YoY (SparkToro)
- VC funding into AEO tools topped $45M in 2025
- Agencies launching dedicated AEO services (NewMedia, GreenBanana SEO)

**The play:**
- Package AEO as a standalone service ($2-5K/month)
- Target: B2B SaaS, professional services, healthcare, financial services (highest need industries)
- Lead with the "free AEO audit" (your checklist + manual review)

**Pricing model:**
- AEO Audit: Free (lead gen)
- AEO Setup: $3-5K one-time
- AEO Retainer: $2-4K/month

### 4. **Revive Dead Leads with Value-First Outreach**
You have old leads that went cold. Most agencies let them die.

**The play:**
- Send them something valuable for FREE (no ask)
- Example: "Hey [Name], I put together a quick AEO audit for [Company]. Thought you'd find it useful. No call needed â€” just wanted to share."
- 20% will reply. Some will convert.

**Why it works:** No one does this. It's unexpected. Creates reciprocity.

### 5. **Strategic Partnerships (Fastest Path to Revenue)**
You don't need to find clients yourself.

**The play:** Find people who already have your clients as their clients:
- Web design agencies (they build sites, can't do performance marketing)
- CRM consultants (HubSpot, Salesforce partners)
- Sales trainers / consultants
- Business coaches

**Offer:** 15-20% referral fee for any deal they send.

**Execution:** Message 10 this week. Keep it simple: "Hey, I work with B2B companies on performance marketing. If you ever have clients asking about paid ads or lead gen, I'll pay you 20% of the first 3 months. Interested?"
