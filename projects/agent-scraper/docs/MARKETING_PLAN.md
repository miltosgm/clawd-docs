### 4.1 SEO (Primary Channel)

**Why SEO First:**
- Low competition for Cyprus real estate keywords
- Long-term sustainable traffic
- High-intent searchers
- â‚¬0 cost (time investment only)

**Target Keywords:**

| Keyword | Volume | Difficulty | Priority |
|---------|--------|------------|----------|
| real estate agents cyprus | 500/mo | Low | High |
| best real estate agents limassol | 200/mo | Low | High |
| cyprus property agents reviews | 100/mo | Very Low | High |
| real estate agencies nicosia | 150/mo | Low | Medium |
| expat real estate cyprus | 100/mo | Low | High |
| [agency name] reviews | Varies | Very Low | High |

**SEO Strategy:**

1. **On-Page Optimization**
   - City pages: /limassol, /nicosia, /paphos, /larnaca
   - Agency pages: /agency/[name]
   - Blog content targeting long-tail keywords

2. **Technical SEO**
   - Fast loading (< 2s)
   - Mobile-first design
   - Schema markup for reviews
   - XML sitemap

3. **Content Strategy**
   - "Best Real Estate Agents in [City]" guides
   - "How to Choose a Real Estate Agent in Cyprus"
   - "Expat Guide to Buying Property in Cyprus"

4. **Link Building**
   - Guest posts on expat blogs
   - HARO responses (property topics)
   - Local business directories

**SEO Timeline:**

| Month | Focus |

| 1 | Technical setup, city pages live |

| 3 | Link building begins, rankings improving |

## 6. Review Acquisition Strategy

### How to Get First 100 Reviews

| Source | Target | Method |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Aggregated reviews | 800+ | Import from Google/Facebook |
| Agency-driven | 30 | Agencies ask past clients |
| Organic | 20 | Users discover and review |
| Incentivized | 50 | Contest/giveaway |

### Review Collection Tactics

1. **Make It Easy**
   - Simple 3-step review form
   - Mobile-friendly
   - No account required (email verification only)

2. **Ask at the Right Time**
   - Email sequence to users who contacted agencies
   - "How was your experience?" prompt

3. **Incentive Programs**
   - Launch contest: "Review your agent, win â‚¬50"
   - Monthly prize draw for reviewers
   - Badge for verified reviewers

4. **Agency Partnership**
   - Provide agencies with review request templates
   - QR codes for print materials
   - Email signature links
